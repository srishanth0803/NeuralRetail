# Churn Model Interpretation

## Key Drivers of Churn

1. Recency is the strongest predictor of churn.
2. Customers inactive for long periods have higher churn probability.
3. Higher purchase frequency reduces churn risk.
4. Customers with higher monetary value are generally more loyal.
5. Frequency decay is an early warning signal for churn.

## Business Recommendations

* Target customers with Recency > 90 days using reactivation campaigns.
* Provide loyalty rewards for declining-frequency customers.
* Prioritize high-value customers for retention offers.
* Create personalized promotions for at-risk segments.

## Expected Impact

Reducing churn among high-risk customers can improve retention and increase customer lifetime value.
